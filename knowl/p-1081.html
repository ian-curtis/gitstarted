So you really to delete a repository? Think long and hard about this one. Are you sure? Are you really sure? Deleting repos are irreversible and can have lasting effects. For instance, all forks will be broken, but not deleted. If you delete a private repo, all forks will also be deleted. Any website that contains a link to your repo will no longer work and people might wonder where it went. And so on.
