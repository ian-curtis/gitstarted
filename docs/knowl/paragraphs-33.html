Billing & plans.

GitHub offers different paid plans 34 . Each of the plans has its own benefits (e.g., more storage, more collaborators) but the GitHub essentials work perfectly fine under the free plan and many people do not upgrade. Once you become more familiar with Git, GitHub, and start developing more projects, you might like an upgrade. However, there is no need to pay anything at this point.

Bonus! Students get free GitHub Pro and access to a multitude of extra deals with their Student Developer Pack 35 . Teachers 36  and Schools 37  can also find benefits (some require a payment).
